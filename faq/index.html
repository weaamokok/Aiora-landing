About Glowr

Glowr is an AI-powered daily schedule app that builds a fully personalized glow-up routine from a single photo. Powered by Google Gemini, it generates your complete daily plan — morning habits, focus blocks, stretch breaks, hydration reminders, and an evening wind-down — all tailored to your lifestyle and goals. No templates. No generic advice. A plan built specifically for you.
A glow up is a personal transformation — physical, mental, and lifestyle — where you intentionally build better habits, improve your wellbeing, boost your confidence, and become the best version of yourself. Glowr gives you the daily roadmap to make that transformation real through consistent, structured daily action.
Glowr is for anyone who wants to build better daily habits but doesn't know where to start — or has started before but struggled to stay consistent. Whether you're a student, professional, parent, or athlete, Glowr builds a schedule that fits your specific life. It's available in 8 languages and used by people worldwide.
Most habit apps give you a blank checklist and expect you to figure out what to put in it. Glowr actually builds your schedule for you using AI. You also get Glowr Buddy (a built-in focus timer with automatic stretch and hydration breaks), a visual calendar view, a monthly shopping list, and support for 8 languages — all in one free app.

How It Works

You upload a clear selfie and answer a few questions about your lifestyle, current habits, goals, and available time. Glowr's AI (Google Gemini) analyzes all of this information and generates a personalized daily schedule calibrated to your starting point and objectives — not a one-size-fits-all template.
Your Glowr schedule includes: a morning routine with habits specific to your goals, timed deep work or focus blocks, guided stretch breaks, hydration reminders, goal-specific daily tasks, and an evening wind-down routine. Every element is generated by AI based on your photo and lifestyle details.
Yes. After your AI schedule is created, you can edit tasks, adjust timing, reorder your day, and remove anything that doesn't fit. The AI gives you a strong, personalized starting point — you refine it to make it perfect for your life.
Glowr Buddy is a built-in focus session timer. It runs timed work sessions with automatic stretch break and hydration reminders between each session — like a Pomodoro timer with wellness built in. It keeps you productive without burning out or forgetting to take care of your body.
MindSpace is Glowr's monthly shopping list feature. It curates a personalized list of supplements, skincare products, and wellness essentials based on your routine — so you never run out of the things your habits depend on.

Privacy & Security

Yes. Your photo is processed securely via Google Gemini's API solely to generate your schedule. We do not store your photos on our servers after processing, and we do not share your image with any third parties. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Glowr collects the lifestyle information you provide during onboarding and your in-app activity data to improve personalization. We do not sell your data to third parties. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.
Yes. You can request account and data deletion at any time by contacting us at support@glowr.app. We process deletion requests within 30 days.

Pricing & Availability

Yes — Glowr is completely free to download and use on Android. Core features including AI schedule generation, Glowr Buddy focus sessions, stretch and hydration reminders, and the calendar view are all free. Optional premium features for advanced personalization are available as upgrades.
Glowr is currently available on Android via the Google Play Store. An iOS version is in active development. Follow us on social media for updates on the iOS launch.
Glowr supports: English, Arabic (العربية), French (Français), Spanish (Español), Russian (Русский), Turkish (Türkçe), and Kiswahili. More languages are being added regularly.
