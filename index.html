Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Aiora before you download.

Aiora is an AI-powered daily schedule app that builds a fully personalized glow-up routine from a single photo. Upload your selfie, share a few details about your lifestyle and goals, and Aiora's AI (powered by Google Gemini and Groq AI) generates a complete daily plan — morning habits, focus blocks, stretch breaks, hydration reminders, and an evening wind-down. No templates. No generic advice. A plan built for you.
Aiora uses Google Gemini or Groq AI to analyze your photo alongside your answers about your lifestyle, current habits, goals, and available time. The AI combines these signals to generate a daily schedule that's genuinely customized to your starting point and targets — not a one-size-fits-all template that everyone else gets too.
Yes — Aiora is completely free to download and use on Android. You can generate your AI schedule, use Aiora Buddy focus sessions, get stretch and hydration reminders, and access the calendar view at no cost. Premium features for advanced personalization and analytics are available as optional upgrades.
A glow up is a personal transformation — physical, mental, and lifestyle — where you intentionally build better habits, improve your appearance, boost your energy, sharpen your focus, and become the best version of yourself. Aiora gives you the daily roadmap to make that transformation real through consistent, structured action instead of vague intentions.
Most habit apps give you a blank checklist and expect you to figure out what to put in it. Aiora actually builds your schedule for you using AI. You also get Aiora Buddy (a built-in focus timer with automatic stretch and hydration breaks), a visual calendar view, a monthly shopping list, and multi-language support — all in one app, all free.
Yes. Your photo is sent directly from your device to the AI model (Google Gemini or Groq AI) — Aiora stores nothing on its own servers. All your data lives exclusively on your device. Your photo is never retained after the AI processes it. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Aiora Buddy is Aiora's built-in focus session timer. It runs timed deep-work sessions (like a Pomodoro timer) and automatically triggers guided stretch breaks and hydration reminders between sessions. It's designed to keep you productive without burning out — you stay in the zone while your body and mind stay cared for.
Aiora currently supports English, Arabic (العربية), French (Français), Spanish (Español), Russian (Русский), Turkish (Türkçe), and Kiswahili. More languages are being added regularly so everyone can build their best day in the language they think in.
Absolutely. After your AI schedule is generated, you can edit tasks, adjust timings, reorder your day, and skip anything that doesn't fit. The AI gives you a strong, personalized starting point — you refine it to make it perfect for your life.
Aiora is currently available on Android via the Google Play Store. An iOS version for iPhone and iPad is in active development and will be launching soon. You can sign up for early access updates in the meantime.
