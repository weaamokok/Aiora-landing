Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Glowr ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Glowr mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information in connection with providing the Service:

  • Photos: When you use our AI schedule generation feature, you voluntarily upload a photo. This image is transmitted to Google Gemini's API solely to generate your personalized schedule. We do not store your photo on our servers after processing is complete.
  • Lifestyle Information: During onboarding, you provide details about your goals, current habits, schedule, and lifestyle. This information is used to personalize your daily schedule.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the app — features used, schedules completed, streak data — to improve the Service.
  • Device Information: We collect basic device information (device type, operating system version) for technical support and analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Generate and personalize your AI daily schedule
  • Operate and improve the Glowr app
  • Provide customer support
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Send you app-related notifications (with your permission)

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We share data with:

  • Google Gemini API: Your photo and lifestyle information are transmitted to Google's Gemini API to generate your schedule. Google's use of this data is governed by their Privacy Policy.
  • Analytics Providers: We use analytics services to understand app usage patterns. These providers receive anonymized, aggregated data only.

4. Data Retention

Photos are deleted from our systems immediately after schedule generation. Lifestyle and usage data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

7. Security

We implement commercially reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. All data transmission between the app and our servers uses industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS). However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "last updated" date above.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@glowr.app
